Then give each serious rival one page. Their core competence: what can they do exceptionally well? Their value propositions, mapped against market segments: where exactly do you overlap, and where not? Their business model, and whether it is similar to yours or different. Strengths, weaknesses, and at the bottom, the only section that creates value: your response, in three concrete actions. Staple those pages behind your overview map and you have the deliverable this module asks for: a living document called Competitor Analysis. Living is the key word. Competitor analysis takes real time and effort, so budget for it. And it never ends, so revisit it every quarter, and immediately after every shock.
