Module six, the heart of this course. You create value for a customer; a business model is simply how you monetise that value. It sounds technical, but it is where startups are truly made or broken, because the same product with a different revenue logic can be a failure or a category winner. Today you will learn the building blocks, a library of proven patterns with African champions behind each one, a three-step design method, the circular model families where impact and margin meet, and two stress tests that can save your company. Let's design how you get paid.
