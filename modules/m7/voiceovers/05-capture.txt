When the fit signals appear, switch modes: capture the beachhead. Market where your persona actually lives: vernacular radio, WhatsApp groups, market day, the cooperative meeting, the rider stage. Hire against your delivery bottlenecks, because in capture mode, growth is limited by what you can deliver, not what you can sell. Keep building only the features your pilots validated. And create the proof that sells itself. In African community and B2B markets, nothing outsells a visible working installation and a neighbour who earns more because of it. A cold room humming in the market square is the advertisement. A rider who banks an extra dollar a day is the sales force. Become the reference name in your pond, the company everyone in the niche mentions when your problem comes up.
